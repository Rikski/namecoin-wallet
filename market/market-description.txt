Have your Namecoins always with you, in your pocket! You pay by quickly scanning a QR-code. As a merchant, you receive payments reliably and instantly. Namecoin Wallet is the first mobile Namecoin app, and arguably also the most secure!

<b>FEATURES</b>

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet is de-centralized and peer to peer.
• Display of Namecoin amount in NMC, mNMC and µNMC.
• Conversion to and from national currencies.
• Sending and receiving of Namecoin via NFC, QR-codes or Namecoin URLs.
• Address book for regularly used Namecoin addresses.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• Sweeping of paper wallets (e.g. those used for cold storage).
• App widget for Namecoin balance.
